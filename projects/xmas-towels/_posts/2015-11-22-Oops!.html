Everything looked good, initially.

But the regions pointed out by the 2 arrows should be identical, and they are not.

All better!

I cut the weft between 2 warp threads in the middle of the messed-up section, and freed up all of the incorrect warps. The weft ends were secured with bag clips. The warp threads were re-threaded, sleyed, and tied back onto the front apron.
