--- layout: default title: Feta Cheese ---

Loosely based on The Complete Idiot's Guide to Cheese

Ingredients

>Cheese:

  • 1 gallon whole milk
  • 1/2 tsp CaCl2 solution, diluted in 2 Tbsp **distilled**
  • 1/8 tsp mild lipase powder, diluted in 2 Tbsp **distilled** water
  • 1/4 tsp mesophilic culture, 1/4 tsp
  • 1/2 prepared rennet tablet = 1/4 tsp, diluted in 2 Tbsp **distilled** water

Heavy brine:

  • 1 quart hot water
  • 8 oz salt
  • 1.5 tsp CaCl2 **solution**
  • 1–1.5 tsp vinegar

Pickling brine:

  • 1 quart hot water
  • 3 oz flaked salt
  • 3/4 tsp CaCl2 **solution**
  • 1/2 tsp vinegar

Instructions

  • Prepare lipase pwdr. Combine milk with calcium chloride and lipase (all). Gently stir and heat to 90°F in Instant Pot on slow cooker setting. Remove from heat.
  • Sprinkle starter over milk surface and rehydrate 1-2 min. Stir thoroughly.
  • Add rennet and mix for 1 min. Note the time. Determine the time of floculation by floating a plastic container or the presence of curd on a knife. Total time = floculation t * 4; there should be a clean break.
  • Cut the cheese into 1/2-inch curds, let sit 5 min.
  • Maintain temp. at 90°F and stir gently for 20min. Allow to settle for 10 min.
  • Drain whey and scoop curd into a towel/basket.
  • Add weight and let sit 30 min.
  • Turn the cheese over and let sit 30 min.
  • Repeat 7 & 8 with 60 min each.
  • Prepare heavy brine and keep at r.t.
  • Remove cheese, cover loosely with cheesecloth, and dry overnight at r.t.
  • The next day, cut cheese into 1-2 inch cubes and place into heavy brine. Store in refrig. for 6 hours. Prepare pickling brine and store in refrig.
  • Place cheese into 1 quart jars, fill with pickling brine, and store in refrig.
